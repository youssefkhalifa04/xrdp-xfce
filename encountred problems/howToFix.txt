1. WSL problem 
    wsl --unregister
    wsl --unistall 
    wsl --install 
(You have to make all these steps every time this error appears to you)
2. Port 3389 is already in use 
    For this problem you have to diffrent senarios : 
    The first option is to kill the service that is listening to the port 3389
    The second option is to make a light change in the command and just choose any other port that is not in service
